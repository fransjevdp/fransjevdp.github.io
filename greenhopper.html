GREENHOPPER

Greenhopper is a platform that i created at my university BAU for my master with 3 co-students. At Greenhopper you can book your transport, while we motivate you to take the most sustainable option. Afterwards you can compensate your emisions with one of our sustainable actions. We iterated this platform 4 times to improve the usability.
