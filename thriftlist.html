THRIFT LIST

For a project at my university ELISAVA in Barcelona I made a shopping guide. THRIFT LIST makes it easy to find nice shops and markets, where you can buy second hand clothing. I created this guide to motivate people to buy ‘slow fashion’ instead of fast fashion.
